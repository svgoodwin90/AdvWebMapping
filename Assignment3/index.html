UBER AFTER DARK
Pickups* from the hours of 8:00pm to 5:00am (sunset and sunrise) on May 18th, 2014 in New York City are representative of typical after-hours, springtime, activity for the transportation company. The chosen date corresponds to the daily median of total rides logged for the month of May.
*Data sourced from a directory of over 4.5 million Uber pickups in New York City from April to September 2014, and 14.3 million from January to June 2015, obtained by FiveThirtyEight via the NYC Taxi & Limousine Commission (TLC) as requested through Freedom of Information Law request on July 20, 2015. Read more and find this data at: https://github.com/fivethirtyeight/uber-tlc-foil-response
